GenerMEDIA
GenerMEDIA is an app that allows family and friends connect with one another with their favorite songs, movies, stories, and historical events from across different generations.
Start
The music section of the app allows you to play and listen to music. You can upload songs that you already have saved onto your device or convert YouTube music videos into mp3 files that are saved to the app. Playlists can be created so you can listen to a favorite list of songs. You are also able to search and view song reccomendations. You can even share the songs you like with the members of your group and save the songs they send you.
The game part of the app allows you to play classic games and watch demos of newer ones. The app also allows you to save the games you like to your device so you can play it later. You can also reccomend games to the other memebers of your group and view the ones they have sent to you. There is even a search function and reccomendation list to help you find even more games you might enjoy.
With the video app, you’re able to share movies and videos that are saved on your phone or found online through a YouTube link with the members of your group. The movies and videos are sorted into two different playlists depending upon what type they are. You are able to search for different videos or movies that have been uploaded by you and your group, and along with others uploaded by other groups that share similar interests as yours. If you like a certain video or movie, you’re able to save it to the app and be able to watch it again later. There is also a button that allows you to view recommendations. If there’s something you don’t like, you can tell it that you’re not interested in that movie/video, or the theme of it.
The History part of the app let’s you view historical newsletters, view photos of historical artifacts taken by group and family members, and video documentations of them discussing their account of a historical event they have witnessed. A search button is present so you can discover documentations about events that you are interested in learning about. If you like a certain historical event, you can share it with your group and save it to your list of favorite articles. If you want to upload something, you’re able to take photos of historical objects you own, record your documentations from a recent or historical event, and upload it onto the app to share with your group and many others.
